What I am into

Games, games, games! No doubt many (if not all of you) also play video games. I love video games, ever since I was a child and my parents bought me a NES, Ive been hooked. Now a days, most of my video games are on my PC but I still own a Nintendo Switch for console games. My current list of games that I try to play weekly are:

  1. Genshin Impact
  2. Apex Legends
  3. Fortnite
  4. Final Fantasy Brave Exvius: War of the Visions

I also am interested in tabletop games. I used to play D&D and Vampire: The Masquerade when I was younger. And although I do not play these any longer, I urge others to give em a try to experience role-playing in a tabletop setting. These days my tabletop games are more army war based rather than adventuring parties. I have sizable armies for the following games:

  1. Warhammer 40,000 - Deathguard (rules!)
  2. Warmachine and Hordes - Retribution of Scyrah
  3. Warcaster: Neo-Mechanika - Iron Star Alliance
Sadly, I am way behind on painting my minatures and do not have anything presentable I can post here yet :(
